# Zotikos Master Roadmap

## 1. Vision

Zotikos designs deterministic, governable enterprise AI and distributed systems using ontology, FSM orchestration, control planes, and typed actions.

The goal is to help regulated enterprises build AI-enabled systems that are explainable, auditable, operationally safe, and simple enough to maintain.

---

### 2.1 Deterministic AI

- Human-in-the-loop systems
- Typed actions
- Policy enforcement
- Auditability

- LLMs treated as untrusted components

### 2.2 Control-Plane Driven Orchestration

- FSM orchestration
- Event-driven systems

- Clear state ownership

### 2.3 Ontology as Contract

- Semantic models
- Enterprise object relationships

### 2.4 Governable Distributed Systems

### 2.5 Operational Simplicity

- LLM as an Untrusted Component

- Typed Actions over Prompt Chaos
- Deterministic Orchestration over Event Chaos
- Semantic Control Plane for Enterprise AI

### 3.2 Positioning Statement

Zotikos helps regulated enterprises build deterministic and governable AI systems for real-world operational environments.

### 3.3 Practical Meaning

Zotikos does not position AI as magic.

Zotikos positions AI as one component inside a controlled enterprise architecture where:

- Business meaning is captured through ontology
- Decisions are constrained by policy
- Actions are typed and auditable
- Workflows are controlled by state machines
- Humans remain in the governance loop where needed
